Loyalty

Send up the red flags and alarms whenever you hear this word. 10’ll get you 20, it’s a scam of some sort. Sniff around.

There are only two things to be loyal to in this world: your self, and your integrity. And usually, if you lose one, the other goes along with it.

If any other “loyalty” seems to collide with those two—family, party, country, job, what-have-you—you’re in trouble. Drop it like a bad habit. Run fast. That ship is sinking.

In other words, “I got only two things in this world: my word, and my balls. And I don’t break ’em for nobody.”
